🐟
Omega-3 Fish Oil 1300mg
Nature's Best Maximum Strength EPA/DHA
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 1 capsule with breakfast and 1 capsule with your evening meal (split dosing improves absorption and reduces fishy aftertaste). On gym days: morning dose only — second dose post-workout evening meal.
🔴 Dose
2 × 1300mg capsules per day (split AM/PM). Provides ~780mg EPA + 520mg DHA total. Research supports 1–3g EPA+DHA daily for cardiovascular, anti-inflammatory and muscle recovery benefits.
🔵 Take With Food
Always take with a fat-containing meal (eggs, avocado, nuts, olive oil). Fat in the meal triggers bile release which dramatically boosts EPA/DHA absorption by up to 50%.
⚠️ Warning & Interactions
  • High-dose fish oil (3g+) may mildly increase bleeding time — inform your doctor if on blood thinners.
  • Do not take at the same time as Vitamin E supplements (separate by 2+ hours).
  • Avoid taking alongside iron supplements — Omega-3 may impair iron absorption.
  • Freeze or refrigerate capsules to prevent oxidation and rancidity.
☀️
Vitamin D3 4000 IU + K2 100mcg
Oxford Origins D3/K2 MK-7 — 240 Capsules
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 1 capsule every morning with breakfast. D3 is fat-soluble — morning dosing aligns with natural cortisol rhythm and does not disrupt evening melatonin production. Take daily, 7 days a week.
🔴 Dose
4000 IU Vitamin D3 + 100mcg K2 (MK-7) daily. K2 (MK-7 form) is critical to direct calcium to bones and away from arteries when supplementing D3 at this dose.
🔵 Take With Food
Take with a fatty breakfast — eggs, salmon, full-fat yoghurt, avocado on toast, or whole milk. Fat increases D3 absorption by 32–57%.
⚠️ Warning & Interactions
  • Do NOT take D3 at the same time as Magnesium — separate by at least 4 hours.
  • K2 (MK-7) interacts with warfarin/anticoagulants — consult your doctor.
  • Get serum 25-OH vitamin D tested every 6 months to stay in 100–150 nmol/L range.
⚡
Super B Vitamin Complex
VitaBright — Full Spectrum B1 B2 B3 B5 B6 B7 B9 B12
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 1 capsule with breakfast, every morning. B vitamins are water-soluble and energising — morning dosing avoids sleep disruption. Do not take in the evening.
🔴 Dose
1 capsule daily as directed on the VitaBright label. Key B vitamins for training: B3 supports NAD+ and energy; B6 supports amino acid metabolism; B12 supports nerve function and red blood cell production.
🔵 Take With Food
Take with a full breakfast — oats, eggs, whole grain bread, or yoghurt. Food reduces nausea that high-dose B3 can cause and improves absorption of B12 and folate.
⚠️ Warning & Interactions
  • High-dose B3 may cause a harmless "flush" — red skin, warmth, tingling. Resolves in 20–30 min.
  • B vitamins compete with Magnesium and Zinc for absorption — space 2+ hours apart from minerals.
  • Your urine may turn bright yellow — this is riboflavin (B2) and is completely harmless.

Astaxanthin 12mg
Nu U Nutrition — 180 Softgels
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 1 softgel with breakfast daily. Astaxanthin is fat-soluble and best absorbed with the morning fatty meal. Consistent daily dosing builds tissue saturation over 2–4 weeks.
🔴 Dose
12mg daily. Research shows 12mg is optimal for exercise-induced oxidative stress, muscle recovery, skin health and anti-inflammatory effects.

☕
Caffeine 200mg (Pure)
Peak Supps — 360 Capsules | Pre-Cardio Only
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 30–45 minutes before cardio on training days only. Caffeine reaches peak plasma concentration at 30–60 min and significantly enhances aerobic performance, endurance output and fat oxidation during cardio.
🔴 Dose
200mg (1 capsule) — for cardio sessions. Do not exceed 400mg total daily caffeine (including coffee/tea).
⚠️ Warning & Interactions
  • Half-life of caffeine is 5–6 hours — avoid after 2pm if training in the morning or sleep will suffer.
  • Caffeine is a diuretic — increase water intake during cardio by 250–500ml.
  • Cycle caffeine: 5 days on, 2 days off (rest days) to prevent tolerance.
  • Do NOT combine with Nicotinamide on detox days — combined vasodilation may cause headaches.
💪
Citrulline Malate 2:1 Powder
Peak Supps — 250g | Pre-Workout Pump & Endurance
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 45–60 minutes before training on all gym days. Earlier timing is essential for full nitric oxide pump effect during lifting.
🔴 Dose
6–8g Citrulline Malate (2:1) per session. Scoop and mix in 300–400ml water.
⚠️ Warning & Interactions
  • May cause a mild headache at first use — start with 4g and increase over 1–2 weeks.
  • Do not combine with PDE5 inhibitors — combined vasodilation may cause dangerous blood pressure drop.
  • Avoid taking with high-dose arginine supplements — redundant and can cause GI upset.
🔥
Beta-Alanine CarnoSyn® Powder
HSN Raw Series CarnoSyn® — 150g
▼
🟢 Timing
Take 30–45 minutes before training on gym days. On rest days: take with any meal. Consistent daily dosing matters most.
🔴 Dose
3.2–6.4g per day (split into 2 × 1.6–3.2g doses) to minimise paraesthesia. CarnoSyn® is the patented, research-validated form.
⚠️ Warning & Interactions
  • Paraesthesia (tingling/flushing) is the most common side effect — harmless. Reduce dose if intolerable.
  • Beta-alanine competes with taurine for cellular uptake — separate by 2+ hours if using taurine.
  • Do not take within 2 hours of the Sauna Detox Nicotinamide dose — combined flushing can be very uncomfortable.

Creatine Monohydrate Creapure®
Bulk.com — The Gold Standard Creatine
▼
🟢 Timing
Post-workout is marginally superior — take within 30 min of finishing training with your protein shake and carbs. On rest days: take with any meal. Take 7 days per week.
🔴 Dose
5g daily (maintenance dose). No loading phase required — steady 5g/day reaches full muscle saturation in 28 days. Creapure® is 99.99% pure creatine monohydrate.

  • On Sauna Detox days: take creatine AFTER the detox window (2+ hours post-sauna) with electrolytes and Vitamin C.
